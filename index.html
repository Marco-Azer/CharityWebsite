About

Our Mission

The Closet group is a non-profit organization, intiated by Virgin Mary Scouts Team(VMS), to solely help unfortunate and deprived families across the globe by providing them with some basic needs like clothes and daily necessities

The Process

We collect simple daily necessities, such as clothes, linens, and basic furniture from the people who don't need it. We categorize the items, ship it and deliver it to the people who desperately need it.

How to help

  1. Prepare the clothes
  2. Call us to schedule a convenient time
  3. Or Drop off the items

Items
